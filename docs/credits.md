## Subject Matter

**The Sarcophagus of Psamtek**

This VR experience features a photogrammetric reconstruction of the basalt sarcophagus belonging to Psamtek, a Late Period official of ancient Egypt. The sarcophagus is part of the archaeological discoveries from the Saqqara necropolis.

### Historical Context

- **Period**: Late Period of Ancient Egypt (664-332 BCE)
- **Location**: Saqqara, Egypt (Memphis necropolis)
- **Official**: Psamtek (Late Period administrator)
- **Material**: Basalt (volcanic stone)
- **Significance**: Example of elite burial practices and hieroglyphic craftsmanship
